    ISO 9001 Quality Management System Consultancy

    ISO 9001 is defined as the international standard that specifies requirements for a quality management system (QMS). Organizations use the standard to demonstrate the ability to consistently provide products and services that meet customer and regulatory requirements.

    “Benefits”

    1. Suitable for both small and large organisations.

    2. Better internal management.

    3. Increase in efficiency, productivity and profit.

    4. Improved customer retention and acquisition.

    5. Consistent outcomes, measured and monitored.

    6. Globally recognized standard.

    7. Compatible with other ISO standards.

    SERVICES OFFERED:

    • Gap Assessment

      • We will conduct a review of your current management system practices, controls and documentation, to determine the extent to which you conform to those required by the specific HACCP, ISO, TS or other standard. An audit report will be presented with our findings and recommendations.

    • Project Planning and Organization

      We work with senior management or the implementation team to:

      • Review the findings and recommendations of the Consultantâ€™s Gap Assessment.

      • Identify, plan and schedule system development and implementation activities.

      • Identify and plan resource requirements for the project.

      • Approve the proposed implementation plan and schedule.

      • Agree on the scope of consulting to be provided.

      • Process Identification and Documentation.

      We help you identify and effectively document all organizational processes relevant to your management system. These include customer-oriented, management, product realization, resource planning, measurement, support and outsourced processes.

    • System Development and Implementation

      We work with your implementation team and process personnel to:

      • Develop and implement adequate controls and documentation for effective planning operation and control of all management system processes.

      • Create management system awareness at all levels within your organization.

      • Ensure methods for interaction and communication between processes are defined, understood and effective.

      • Establish and track appropriate performance objectives for key processes. We provide guidance on achieving these objectives.

      • Ensure appropriate records are maintained for all processes.

      • We use proven tools and techniques to help you develop a management system that can be certified in the shortest possible time as well as contribute to your bottom line in improved operational performance.

    • Management System Documentation

      We help you develop and in many instances provide hands-on assistance in preparing your haccp, quality or food safety manual, procedures, work instructions, forms, checklists and other operational documents. We will work closely with your personnel in this activity as it is important that they understand, use and take ownership for these documents and implementation.

    • Documentation Review

      If you have completed your documentation and need an expert review before you submit it to your Certification Body (CB), we provide this service as well. We make sure that all your documents conform to requirements.

    • Pre-Assessment

      • Once the management system has been fully implemented and sufficient records exist to demonstrate effective performance, we will conduct a pre-assessment audit to determine whether your management system is ready for a formal audit by an accredited Certification Body.

      • We will present an Audit Report and work with your staff to determine appropriate corrective action for any non-conformance found.

      • At this time we will also make your employees familiar with the certification audit process and provide guidance on handling external audit interviews.

    • Support during the Certification Audit Process

      Early in the implementation schedule we will help you select an appropriate Certification Body. During the certification audit, we can arrange to be on site to provide guidance in addressing any issues or corrective action required by the external auditor. We guarantee customer satisfaction and we will work with you until your organization is successfully certified.

    • Continual Improvement Process

      Your management system must be a dynamic process to continually improve the effectiveness and efficiency of your organization. We can train your employees in problem-solving skills and techniques to keep this process robust and focused to ensure value-added measurable results and continuity of certification.
